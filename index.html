    Local Junior has Birthday, Peers Rejoice

    Jennifer Crane, Palo Alto High School’s new athletic director, works in her co-taught contemporary world history class during sixth period. This will be the last two weeks of her tenure as a co-teacher for history before her full transition to athletic director. “I think that past experience shows to me that prioritizing the fostering of character, hard work and perseverance is a building block to becoming a successful human being in whatever capacity you define that,” Crane said. “I think it’s something that I will continue to emphasize and to help guide me in this process… All those things take precedence over winning.” (Photo: Maxwell Zhang)

    Atticus Kuhn
    June 1, 2022

    Reports have surfaced that local junior, Avi Srinivasan, is celebrating his 17th birthday.

    Although some critics doubted the veracity because it is hard to believe that he is becoming so old, they have now come to accept the truth.

    Srinivasan, who first entered Paly in 2019, has indelibly left his mark on the campus. His classmates are reportedly "amazed" by the hard work he has put into APUSH and French.

    John Bungarden, the APUSH teacher, confided to the Paly Voice that Srinivasan knows "a lot" about the muckrakers of the Progressive Era.

    The Paly Voice interviewed members of the public to ascertain their opinion on the matter.

    Junior Caden Domingo called in to say, "I wish him a happy birthday, he's a great friend."

    "I'm really happy for him and I hope he has a nice summer" commented Atticus Kuhn, a junior. Kuhn continued, "I think he will be an excellent teacher at his journalism camp"

    After some investigative journalism, the Paly Voice has discovered that Srinivasan is organising a summer journalism camp. He will be mentoring the younger children in how to start a newspaper. The participents will create their own newspaper and learn all the skills needed for journalism such as interviewing and paraphrasing.

    "I think this effort is commendable", exclaimed Max Chang when presented with the evidence of Srinivasan's goodwill. Chang maintained, "I think the kids are lucky to be in such safe hands"

    Reportedly, Srinivasan was spotted in southern California investigating colleges, with the furvour of a good investigative journalist. Wherever he goes, his track record demonstrates that any college will be blessed to accept him.

    Happy Birthday Avi! (from Atticus)
